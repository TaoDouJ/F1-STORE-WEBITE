DESCRIPTION:

The Red Bull RB19 was Oracle Red Bull Racing’s 2023 Formula 1 car, and it became one of the most dominant machines in the sport’s history. Powered by the Honda RBPT 1.6L V6 turbo-hybrid engine, it produced well over 950 horsepower, paired with an ultra-efficient energy recovery system. Built around a carbon-fiber monocoque chassis, the RB19 featured highly refined aerodynamics with ground-effect tunnels, a reworked diffuser, and a sleek rear wing that maximized downforce while reducing drag. Driven by Max Verstappen and Sergio Pérez, the car delivered record-breaking results: 21 wins in 22 races, with Verstappen securing his third consecutive Drivers’ Championship and Red Bull clinching the Constructors’ title with ease. Its classic navy-blue livery with red and yellow accents became instantly iconic, symbolizing a season of near-total dominance.
