DESCRIPTION:

The Racing Bulls VCARB 02 is the Visa Cash App Racing Bulls team’s 2025 Formula 1 car, designed as an evolution of the previous VCARB 01. Built around a carbon-fibre monocoque chassis with Halo protection, it’s powered by the Honda RBPT RBPTH002 1.6L V6 turbo-hybrid engine, paired with an 8‑speed semi-automatic gearbox. Aerodynamically, the car features revised sidepods, a reshaped airbox, and refined ground-effect tunnels to improve airflow and cooling, while still sharing some mechanical components with Red Bull Technologies. Its livery combines a dominant white base with dark blue rear sections, accented by the iconic Red Bull branding. Driven by Yuki Tsunoda, Isack Hadjar, and later Liam Lawson, the VCARB 02 was engineered to be stable, predictable, and easier to set up, giving younger drivers a consistent platform to perform on the ultra-competitive 2025 grid.
