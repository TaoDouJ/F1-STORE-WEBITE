DESCRIPTION:

The Racing Point RP20 was the team’s 2020 Formula 1 car, famously dubbed the “Pink Mercedes” due to its striking resemblance to the title-winning Mercedes W10. Powered by the Mercedes-AMG F1 M11 EQ Performance 1.6L V6 turbo-hybrid engine, it delivered over 950 horsepower and featured many shared components, including the gearbox and rear suspension. The RP20’s aerodynamics were heavily inspired by Mercedes’ 2019 design, leading to controversy but also impressive performance gains. Its pink livery, sponsored by BWT, made it one of the most visually distinctive cars on the grid. Driven by Sergio Pérez and Lance Stroll, the RP20 secured a race win at Sakhir and multiple podiums, finishing 4th in the Constructors’ Championship, marking Racing Point’s most successful season before rebranding as Aston Martin.
