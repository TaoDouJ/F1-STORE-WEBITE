DESCRIPTION:

The Red Bull RB16B was Red Bull Racing’s 2021 Formula 1 car, a refined evolution of the RB16 that brought the team back into title contention. Powered by the Honda RA621H 1.6L V6 turbo-hybrid engine, it delivered blistering performance and reliability, helping Max Verstappen secure his first Drivers’ Championship in a dramatic season finale. The RB16B featured improved aerodynamics, including a reworked floor to adapt to 2021 regulation changes, a tighter rear packaging, and enhanced suspension geometry. Its navy-blue livery with red and yellow accents remained iconic, while the car’s balance and tire management gave it an edge on a variety of circuits. With 11 wins and 18 podiums, the RB16B marked the end of Honda’s official F1 engine supply era on a high note and laid the foundation for Red Bull’s future dominance.
