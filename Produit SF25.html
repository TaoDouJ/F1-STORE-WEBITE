The Ferrari SF-25 is Scuderia Ferrari’s 2025 Formula 1 car, designed to return the team to championship contention. The car introduces a new pull-rod front suspension and refined aerodynamics, featuring slimmer sidepods, a reworked air intake, and a sleeker rear wing for improved efficiency. Its striking livery uses a deeper shade of Ferrari red, and with Charles Leclerc and Lewis Hamilton at the wheel, the SF-25 represents Ferrari’s boldest step forward in the new era of F1.
