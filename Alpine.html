DESCRIPTION:

The Alpine A525 is BWT Alpine F1 Team’s 2025 Formula 1 challenger, developed as an evolution of the A524. It’s powered by the Renault E-Tech RE25 1.6L V6 turbo-hybrid engine, producing over 950 horsepower with a 15,000 rpm rev limit. Built around a carbon-fibre and aluminium honeycomb monocoque, the car features push‑rod suspension at both ends, Brembo carbon brakes with brake‑by‑wire integration, and an 8‑speed semi‑automatic gearbox. Aerodynamically, the A525 refines the sidepod and undercut design while addressing the weight issues of its predecessor. Its striking blue-and-pink livery reflects Alpine’s identity and title sponsor BWT. Driven by Pierre Gasly and Franco Colapinto, the A525 represents Alpine’s bid to climb back up the midfield, blending continuity with targeted upgrades for the 2025 season.
